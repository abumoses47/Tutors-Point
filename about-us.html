About Tutor Point Group

Welcome to the Tutor Point Community, a unique educational network dedicated to connecting eager learners with experienced tutors. Our mission is to go beyond traditional education, offering tutoring services for a wide range of subjects and skills, including Mathematics, English language and grammar, phonetics, pronunciation, software development, and professional exams.

We cater to learners starting from the age of 4 and above, allowing parents or guardians to easily register them online for personalized sessions conducted in the comfort of the learner's home at a convenient time. Our approach encompasses various engaging learning methods, such as one-on-one tutorials, and online sessions for children, and adults. Our community's tutors are all specialists in their respective fields, and we maintain a rigorous verification process to uphold our integrity as the premier private home and online tutoring company in Nigeria. We are dedicated professionals committed to achieving excellence in education.
